# VISITKOSZEG.HU: "The Secret Key" Strategy

## 1. Goal: High Curiosity, Low Information
Focus on generating "FOMO" (Fear Of Missing Out). We don't explain the app; we show the *power* it gives the user. We want people to ask: "Mi ez és hol tudom letölteni?"

## 2. Phase 1: The "Extreme Teaser"
Since the system is not yet active, we avoid naming specific features (Eats, Booking, Tickets). Instead, we focus on the *impact* of the coming technology.

### A. The "Vanish" Concept
*   **Teaser:** "The old ways of visiting Kőszeg are about to disappear. Something faster, deeper, and more personal is coming."
*   **Goal:** Create a sense of an impending upgrade to the city's experience.

### B. The "First Choice" Hook
*   **Teaser:** "Only those who are watching will be the first to hold the key."
*   **Goal:** Build a waitlist mentality without a literal waitlist.

## 3. Merchant Strategy: "The Waitlist"
Instead of pitching to them, we make them want to be part of the "Elite" group.
*   **Hook:** "Valami új érkezik, ami újrarendezheti a kőszegi turizmust. Szeretnéd, ha a te üzleted is látható lenne?"
*   **Tone:** "Korlátozott férőhelyek a béta tesztben."

## 4. The "Visual Wow" Factor
We will use high-end mockups to show the app as a premium product.
*   **Style:** Dark mode, glassmorphism, cinematic photography.
*   **Vibe:** Apple-style aesthetics mixed with Kőszeg heritage.
